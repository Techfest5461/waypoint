About Waypoint

A trip planner built on real trade-offs, not just pretty pictures.

Waypoint scores a hand-researched dataset of Indian destinations against the things that actually decide a trip: what you can spend per day, who you're travelling with, how long you have, when you're going, and — now — how far it realistically is from where you're starting.

Photos on every card are pulled live from Wikipedia the moment you search, using the exact article title stored against each destination — not stored on our end — so the visuals stay accurate and attributed, even though the trip data itself is a curated snapshot rather than a live pricing feed.
